OUR PASSION

Reinventing Street Comfort Food

We are on a mission to elevate fast food. Today, Chacha Wow stands as Ludhiana's favorite sanctuary for gourmet fast food and crafted momos.

Inspired by the spirit of feast and celebration, we believe comfort food should be abundant, fresh, and exciting. We combine traditional street spices with premium ingredients. Every dumpling is folded by hand, every burger patty is seasoned to perfection, and every shake is churned fresh.

"Great food brings people together. We design street-food classics to be a sensory celebration of flavors—crispy, hot, spicy, and satisfying."

Chef Devendra Singh Executive Culinary Director
